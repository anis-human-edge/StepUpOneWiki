# TEDx Amsterdam

[Mohamed Anis](../entities/people/mohamed-anis.md) was selected from 100 applicants to pitch at the TEDx Amsterdam Impact Program in late March / early April 2026. 13 were selected; 8 would be chosen for the final event.

## The Pitch

- **Format:** 3-minute story + 5-minute Q&A
- **Structure:** 6 segments of 30-40 seconds each:
  1. The trigger (inspiration from Muhammad Hassan speaking to Microsoft CEO)
  2. Anis's background (25 years at Infosys, knowing how to solve the challenge)
  3. Starting with 5 people
  4. Realizing training alone is insufficient -- the story of Deng and Fujitsu
  5. The need to focus on all people in the camp, not just one success story
  6. The call to action: "Give us your toughest business challenge. Let us solve it. If it works, we become lifelong partners. If it doesn't, you still give us a chance."

## Strategy Shift

The TEDx opportunity triggered a strategic pivot: the organization shifted primary focus from winning clients to **maximizing exposure**. The reasoning: if the story reaches 1 million founders via the TEDx platform, the resulting trust would lead to completely different (and superior) client opportunities.

## Preparation Process

- AI-assisted speech writing: Anis provided full context to the AI, defined desired audience feelings, stated constraints (no charity narrative), and manually wrote the "heartbeat" for each segment
- Slides: photos only, no text (per AI recommendation)
- Team feedback: rated the story "10 out of 10" and "very touching"
- Specific edit: removed reference to "not wanting laptops" to avoid closing the door on infrastructure help
