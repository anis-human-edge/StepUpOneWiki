# Research Engine

The research engine is the system that identifies signals, targets, and opportunities for both inbound and outbound strategies. It is the single most important component of StepUp.One's go-to-market machine -- "Research is where 10x comes from."

## Architecture

The research engine serves two distinct purposes depending on the strategy:

### For Outbound
Research focuses on understanding individual clients deeply through the KYC framework:
- **KYCP** (Know Your Client as a Person) -- personal details, time zone, passions, vision
- **KYCB** (Know Your Client's Business) -- products, pricing, geography, revenue, team
- **CNT** (Context, Needs, Timing) -- unspoken needs, desperate problems, timing windows
- **KYCTA** (Target Audience) -- the client's own ICP, segmented and prioritized

Inputs: client website, LinkedIn company page, founder's LinkedIn profile, Twitter/Instagram/Facebook, conversation transcripts.

### For Inbound
Research focuses on finding high-value signals for engagement:
- **Source 1: Watch list** -- 100-300 segmented accounts matching the ICP, checked multiple times daily
- **Source 2: Trending topics** -- LinkedIn topic searches, industry news
- **Source 3: Competitor posts** -- what competitors are posting and how audiences engage
- **Source 4: Search intent** -- what target audiences are actively searching for

Daily output: a ranked list of 20-30 engagement opportunities (primarily comment opportunities), from which the team leader selects the top 3-5 for execution.

## AI Integration

The research engine uses AI at two tiers:

| Tier | Cost | Quality | Use Case |
|------|------|---------|----------|
| Premium | $20/client | Extremely high class, deep nuance | High-value client research, strategic planning |
| Economy | $1/client | Good enough, usable | Initial prospecting, high-volume research |

Strategy: use premium AI for advice and direction-setting, economy AI for data collection and execution. In cases where the client deal justifies it (e.g., $100/report premium), the expensive tier is used end-to-end.

## Human Intelligence Layer

A critical finding: AI gets target audience definition wrong 90% of the time without specific human direction. This is the most important human contribution to the research engine. If AI could master target audience definition, clients might bypass StepUp.One entirely -- but it cannot, which is why the human+AI combination is defensible.

## Platform Integration

Mohamed Anis is building a platform that integrates the research engine directly into client management:
- Create a client in the portal
- Spin up a system (outbound, inbound, or both)
- Assign team members and team leaders
- Generate KYC documents one by one through the platform
- Each document feeds into the next, building a comprehensive client profile

The platform was demonstrated in the Apr 13 afternoon session -- built on a local machine, not yet published, but functional enough to create clients, assign teams, and generate documents with "one click."
