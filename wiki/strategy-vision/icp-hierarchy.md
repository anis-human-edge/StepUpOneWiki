# ICP Hierarchy

Three-tier Ideal Client Profile structure used to organize targeting strategy across all StepUp.One client motions.

## Structure

### Mega ICP

The broadest classification of who StepUp.One targets:

| Mega ICP | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Founder | Startup founders at any stage |
| Investor | Angel investors, VCs, fund managers |
| Executive | C-suite and senior leaders |
| Partner | Advisors, coaches, consultants |

### Main ICP

Specific segments within each Mega ICP. Examples for Founders:

- Stealth founders
- Event-attending founders
- Accelerator founders (lake strategy)
- Founders posting on LinkedIn
- First-time founders

### Sub ICP

Geographic, behavioral, or activity-based filters:

- US-based
- Posted on LinkedIn in last 30 days
- Recently funded
- Specific industry verticals

## CRM Implementation

A custom object called "ICP" must be created in the CRM with the following fields:

- **Mega ICP** (Founder/Investor/Executive/Partner)
- **Main ICP** (specific segment name)
- **Sub ICP** (geographic/behavioral filter)
- **Population count** (number of people matching criteria)
- **Sales Navigator URL** (direct link to the saved search)

This structured visibility allows the CEO or any manager to immediately understand the team's targeting strategy and thought process.

## Client-Facing Application

For each client, the ICP hierarchy must be documented in the strategy document (not just the CRM), explaining:

- **What** segment is being pursued
- **Why** this segment was chosen
- **How** the outreach will be executed
- **How many** people exist in the segment

Without this documentation, team members end up with shallow understanding of client products and target audiences -- identified as a fundamental weakness across the team.

## Teaching Example

The "coach trainer" case study from the Mar 19 session: the team had 10,000 contacts in the "professional training and coaching" industry but these were general coaches, not specialized "coach trainers" the client needed. This exposed the gap between broad industry filtering and precise ICP targeting.
