# Portfolio Diagnostics

Silent portfolio health monitoring for investors. Tracks leading indicators across portfolio companies without contacting founders -- identifies distress signals before the emergency board call.

**Audience:** Angel investors, VCs, fund managers with 10-25+ portfolio companies
**Pricing:** $5,000-$10,000/month covering up to 25 portfolio companies
**URL:** https://stepup.one/investors/diagnostics

## The Problem: The Signal Gap

Monthly founder updates are lagging indicators with terrible signal-to-noise ratios. Founders write updates to make themselves look good. By the time a GP realizes a company is in trouble, it is often too late.

- A typical investor knows 3 companies are doing great, thinks 5 are struggling, and is guessing about the other 12
- 60% of startup failures happen silently under the board's radar until the final catastrophic cash crunch
- Distress leaks into public data: sudden freezes in mid-level hiring, anomalous spikes in leadership departures signal capital constraint long before the official board update

## How It Works: The Monitoring Layer

StepUp.One operators strictly track external data signals -- hiring decay, leadership churn, outbound volume -- to construct health snapshots silently. No founder contact required.

### Three Modules

| Module | Function |
|--------|----------|
| **MOD-01: Real-Time Dashboard** | Continuous portfolio health scoring fed by tracking operators mapping hiring velocity, outbound signal, and burn velocity |
| **MOD-02: Preemptive Alerts** | Early warning algorithms trigger intervention protocols when a company crosses critical runway or personnel risk thresholds |
| **MOD-03: Quarterly Deep Dives** | Manual, operator-driven qualitative analysis identifying which companies require immediate GTM rescue or M&A exit mappings |

## Key Principle

Diagnose before you rescue. The diagnostic layer feeds directly into StepUp.One's intervention engines.
