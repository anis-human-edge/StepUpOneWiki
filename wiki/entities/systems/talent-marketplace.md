# Talent Marketplace

Marketplace connecting certified StepUp.One operators with clients who need execution teams. The supply side of StepUp.One's talent flywheel.

**Audience:** Talent (certified operators seeking work), and indirectly clients seeking execution capacity
**URL:** https://stepup.one/talent/marketplace

> **Note:** The source page (client-side rendered) contained only nav/footer text. Content needs manual review or re-scraping with JavaScript rendering enabled.

## Inferred Context

The Talent Marketplace is where StepUp.One's 1,400+ reskilled graduates (from refugee camps and underserved populations) are matched with client engines. When a founder buys a Fundraising Engine or GTM Engine, the operators servicing that engine come from this marketplace. The marketplace connects:

- **Supply:** Graduates who have completed the [Reskilling Engine](reskilling-engine.md) training and [Operator Certification](operator-certification.md)
- **Demand:** Clients buying engines across all segments (Founders, Investors, Executives)

This is the core of StepUp.One's business model -- reskilling the underserved into AI-amplified professionals who deliver real outcomes for paying clients at market rates.
