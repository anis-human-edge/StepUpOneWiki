## Inferred Context

Based on StepUp.One's model, Operator Certification is the bridge between training (via the [Reskilling Engine](reskilling-engine.md) and [Daily Learning sessions](../../training-reskilling/teaching-methodology.md)) and paid work (via the [Talent Marketplace](talent-marketplace.md)). Operators are the "AI-amplified professionals" referenced across all engine pages -- the human team members who run daily outreach, manage pipelines, audit AI outputs, and ensure brand safety for clients.

The certification likely covers:

- Brand safety and human-in-the-loop protocols
- Pipeline management and reporting
