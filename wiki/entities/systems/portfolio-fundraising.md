# Portfolio Fundraising (Plan B)

Parallel fundraising execution across an investor's entire portfolio. StepUp.One runs independent fundraising pipelines for multiple portfolio companies simultaneously using the same two-layer methodology as the [Fundraising Engine](fundraising-engine.md).

**Audience:** Angel investors, VCs, syndicate leads, fund managers, accelerators
**Pricing:** $400/month per company per engine, cancel anytime, no contracts, no setup fees
**URL:** https://stepup.one/plan-b-fundraising

## Core Thesis

90% of startups never raise their next round -- not because the product failed, but because nobody was building investor relationships while the founders were building product. An investor cannot personally run outreach for 10 portfolio companies. StepUp.One is "Plan B" -- running in parallel to whatever founders are already doing.

## Who This Is For

- **Angel investors & VCs** wanting to proactively protect existing bets and ensure portfolio survival
- **Syndicate leads** looking to increase aggregate portfolio survival rates
- **Fund managers** who cannot personally intro every founder to every right-fit investor
- **Accelerators** whose founders urgently need fundraising execution immediately after the programme ends

## How It Works

Full system per company: strategy, targeting, founder profile, pitch messaging, digital twin, and a shared operational team running daily execution.

### Layer 1 -- Foundation (Weeks 1-2 per company)

Same 6-step foundation as the individual [Fundraising Engine](fundraising-engine.md), applied independently per company:
1. Fundraising strategy assessed company-by-company (stage, target raise, ideal investor profile, engine selection)
2. Investor targeting (fund, partner, timing levels) -- per company
3. Profile transformation for each founder's LinkedIn
4. Custom messaging architecture per company and investor
5. AI digital twin per founder (trained on their unique voice, pitch deck, communication tone)
6. Shared team across portfolio with resources allocated per company based on activity level

### Layer 2 -- Engine (Week 3+ per company)

Same 4-phase cycle (Connect, Engage, Pitch, Nurture) running independently for each portfolio company, with the same three engine types available:

| Engine | Volume | Best For |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Relationship Led | 200 investors/month | Companies building toward a raise next year |
| Intent Led | 100 investors/month | Targeting investors who backed the same niche |
| Signal Led | 100 investors/month | Catching live deployment signals (new funds, exits, announcements) |

## What the Investor Gets

- Portfolio-level dashboard showing pipeline health per company
- Weekly reports per company: connections, conversations, meetings booked
- Visibility into which companies are gaining investor traction and which are not
- Flexibility to add or remove companies month to month
- Transparent dashboard as single source of truth (no overlap with founder's warm network)
- Confidence that Plan B is running while founders build product

## Anti-Overlap Protocol

All outreach runs on an assigned target list. The system strictly never overlaps with the founder's warm network. The portfolio dashboard acts as the single source of truth.
