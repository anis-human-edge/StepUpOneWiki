# Reskilling Engine

StepUp.One's core social impact engine. Trains people in refugee camps and underserved communities to deliver enterprise-grade work for global clients using AI tools. Not charity -- professional employment at market rates.

**URL:** https://stepup.one/reskilling

> **Note:** The source page (client-side rendered) contained only nav/footer text. Details below are synthesized from the homepage and other pages.

## Origin Story (from homepage)

In 2019, StepUp.One bet on training people in refugee camps to deliver enterprise-grade work for global clients. Started with 5 people in Kakuma refugee camp, Kenya. Today: 1,400+ graduates across 7+ camps.

### AI Timeline
- **2022:** Introduced Gen AI into training
- **2023:** RAG-based AI systems
- **2024:** Context infrastructure platform
- **2025:** Agentic AI

The teams are not cheap labour. They are AI-amplified professionals who deliver outcomes for paying clients at market rates. "No pity. No handouts. Just work."

## Stats
- 1,400+ refugee graduates
- 7+ camps
- 1,000+ clients served
- 30+ countries
- 7 years active

## The Flywheel

The Reskilling Engine is the supply side of StepUp.One's entire business model:

1. **Train** via the Reskilling Engine and [Daily Learning sessions](../../training-reskilling/teaching-methodology.md)
2. **Certify** via [Operator Certification](operator-certification.md)
3. **Deploy** via the [Talent Marketplace](talent-marketplace.md)
4. **Deliver** via client engines ([Fundraising](fundraising-engine.md), [GTM](gtm-engine.md), [Talent Acquisition](talent-acquisition-engine.md), etc.)
