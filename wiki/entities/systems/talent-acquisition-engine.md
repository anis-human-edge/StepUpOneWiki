# Talent Acquisition Engine

Enterprise-grade candidate sourcing operation for founders and scaling companies. Flat-fee alternative to recruitment agencies (no 20% placement fees). Daily passive sourcing builds a warm bench of talent.

**Audience:** Founders hiring first 5-50 employees, scaling companies, CTOs, anti-agency buyers
**Pricing:** $400/month per engine, cancel anytime, no contracts, no setup fees (0% agency fees)
**URL:** https://stepup.one/talent-acquisition

## Core Thesis

Most companies hire reactively: a role opens, they post on boards, they wait. The best candidates never apply because they are not looking. Passive talent requires engaging before pitching -- finding them on GitHub, StackOverflow, and LinkedIn, building familiarity, so when you reach out, they respond. That takes hours a day. StepUp.One does it daily.

## How It Works: Two Layers

### Layer 1 -- Build the Foundation (Weeks 1-2)

1. **Hiring Strategy** -- Define who you need. Select engine: Relationship Led (warm bench), Intent Led (job-change signals), Signal Led (active engagement).
2. **Candidate Targeting** -- Candidate-by-candidate mapping. Skill level (technologies, frameworks, history), activity level (commits, SO answers, LinkedIn posts), timing level (job anniversaries, layoffs, role changes). Every candidate scored before pipeline entry.
3. **Employer Profile** -- Rebuild company page (signals culture/credibility), hiring manager profile (shows technical depth/vision), content (why you are worth a conversation).
4. **Messaging Architecture** -- Not generic InMails. Connect (reference specific commits/projects), educate (share engineering culture), pitch (tailored to what they care about). A/B tested sequences. The outreach ingests the CTO's technical writing and the candidate's actual codebase activity.
5. **Recruiter Digital Twin** -- Trained on employer brand voice, matches hiring manager's tone, generates messages and engaging posts. Scale without losing authenticity.
6. **Team Deployment** -- Strategist (owns hiring pipeline), sourcing specialist (runs daily cycle), brand & AI (manages employer brand and twin).

### Layer 2 -- Run the Engine (Week 3+)

| Phase | Action | Principle |
|-------|--------|-----------|
| P1: Connect | Follow top-tier candidates, like technical posts, comment on commits/projects/SO answers, connect with personalised context | Recognition before permission |
| P2: Engage | Daily engagement. Monitor signals (new posts, job anniversaries, OSS activity). Comment meaningfully on technical posts. React to movement signals. | Familiarity builds trust |
| P3: Pitch | Only after recognition established. Share something specific about the role/technical problem. Pitch referencing skills and fit. Book screening calls. | Context before ask |
| P4: Nurture | Build a warm bench. Post employer brand content. Nurture candidates on structured cadence. Flag interested candidates when roles open. | Stop starting from zero |

## Three Engine Types

| Engine | Volume | Best For | Approach |
