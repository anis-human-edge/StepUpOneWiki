# Mohamed Anis

Founder and CEO of StepUp.One. Leads the Daily Learning sessions, designs the go-to-market systems, builds the internal technology platform, and is the primary teacher and strategic decision-maker.

## Role

- **Email:** anis@human-edge.io / anis@human-edge.ai
- **Position:** CEO, StepUp.One
- **Location:** London, UK
- **Background:** Former Infosys professional; extensive experience selling to Fortune 500 C-level executives in New York

## Teaching Style

Mohamed Anis runs every Daily Learning session as a live masterclass. He builds systems in real time, reviews client interactions line by line, and uses public correction as a teaching tool: "I always take the opportunity to nail the person -- not to nail you, but to teach everybody else."

He is direct about the difficulty of what he asks: projecting authority in sales when you come from nothing. He shares his own vulnerability -- "The first 10 meetings I did, I was begging just like you guys. My heart was outside my body" -- while insisting the team can and must learn the same confidence.

## Philosophy

- **No repetition:** Sessions don't repeat material. "We move like a train."
- **Human + AI:** The combination of human talent and AI capability is StepUp.One's defensible advantage. "Nobody can steal it. They can steal the AI, but they need the humans."
- **Concise communication:** "Choose concise for all your posts. Detailed is too much."
- **Authority over desperation:** The central sales lesson he teaches across every session.
- **Technology for the underserved:** Committed to using cutting-edge AI (better than 90% of London startups) for populations that normally have no access to it.

## System Building

Mohamed Anis personally builds the [Client Management Platform](../systems/client-management-platform.md), writes the outbound and inbound "bibles," and develops the AI-powered research engine. He built the client portal in "3-4 hours" on Apr 13, 2026, demonstrating it live to the team.
