# Hamza Ahmed

Team member at StepUp.One focused on CRM dashboards and opportunity tracking.

## Role

- **Position:** Team member, CRM dashboard development
- **Lake assignment:** Google for Startups

## Key Contributions

- Created an Excel-based opportunities dashboard showing 100 opportunities by team member, which Mohamed Anis praised for quality but redirected to be built inside the CRM for sustainability
- Learned CRM dashboard configuration in a live session, working through widget selection, grouping by assignee, and filtering by pipeline stage
- Shared the components of the new AI twin system, documenting the outreach (connect, engage, pitch, close) and follow-up (warm light, value drop, trigger, social proof, breakups) sections

## Teaching Moment

Hamza's manual data entry into Excel became a key teaching moment about sustainable leadership: building systems that auto-update rather than manually maintaining external dashboards that will inevitably be abandoned.
