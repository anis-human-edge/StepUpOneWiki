# Farah Ibrahim

Senior team leader at StepUp.One. One of the most vocal and experienced team members, often serving as a bridge between Mohamed Anis and the broader team.

## Role

- **Email:** farah.ibrahim@stepup.one
- **Position:** Team Leader
- **Tenure:** 5+ years at StepUp.One
- **Responsibilities:** Win-back lost clients (CRM-based, one-on-one), team leadership, session facilitation when Mohamed Anis is unavailable

## Notable Contributions (Apr 13-15, 2026)

- **Challenged system-building time allocation:** Told Mohamed Anis that the 11-12 meeting time should be spent on client work, not building systems. "Whatever you are doing is not adding any value to our clients." Mohamed Anis agreed to build systems on his own time.
- **Led the Apr 15 session:** When Mohamed Anis left for a London client meeting, Farah took over the session. He led a structured reflection on the outbound document, defined terminology, and facilitated team discussion.
- **Questioned meeting decline:** Confirmed that moving from founder targeting to investor targeting caused the decline in meetings, as predicted. Advocated reverting to founders.
- **Supported authority principle:** Asked the team, "Is there anybody who begged and got a client?" -- reinforcing the authority-over-desperation teaching.
- **Outbound priorities review:** Walked the team through win-new, serve-current, grow-existing, win-back motions during the led session. Emphasized that the two key metrics are opportunities and meetings.

## Perspective

Farah Ibrahim is described by Mohamed Anis as fearless in his communication -- willing to disagree openly and serve as "the voice" for team members who keep quiet when they disagree. Mohamed Anis explicitly values this: "I really want the same character for everybody."

Farah also pushes for practical application over theory: "We built thousands of systems in front of us. Our notion has enough for a master's thesis. But we're not linking it to our work."
