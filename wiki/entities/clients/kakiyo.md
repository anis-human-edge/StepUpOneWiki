# Kakiyo

AI-powered outreach automation tool used by StepUp.One for LinkedIn prospecting and follow-up messaging.

## Contact

- **Ilan Asseo** -- Support (ilan.asseo@kakiyo.intercom-mail.com)

## Relationship

Kakiyo is a **vendor**, not a client. StepUp.One uses Kakiyo's platform for automated LinkedIn outreach campaigns.

## Support Thread (Mar 2026)

[Mohamed Anis](../people/mohamed-anis.md) opened a support ticket (#81087080) about follow-up messages not firing despite client connections and no replies. Key exchanges:

- **2026-03-06:** Ilan acknowledged the issue, escalated to tech team
- **2026-03-10:** Ilan offered to give back a week if downtime was confirmed
- **2026-03-17:** Ilan explained Kakiyo cannot read previous conversations with prospects -- fresh prospects are recommended for campaigns
- **2026-03-17:** Ilan confirmed the feature to read prior conversations is on the roadmap (potentially within weeks) but raised privacy concerns
- **2026-03-17:** Anis asked about 200 messages/day capacity. Ilan responded this would be "too much" but confirmed it is on the roadmap

## Implications for Operations

The 200 msg/day limit and inability to read prior conversations are constraints that affect the team's outbound capacity. The team uses Kakiyo alongside the AI digital twin system for the outreach portion (connect, engage, pitch, close) of client acquisition.
