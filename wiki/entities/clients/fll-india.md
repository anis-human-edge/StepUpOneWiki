# FIRST LEGO League India

Strategic initiative to relaunch FIRST LEGO League (FLL) in India, proposed by StepUp.One to FIRST, the IET, and LEGO.

## Key Contacts

- **Tom Hall** -- LEGO (tom.hall@lego.com). Forwarded the proposal to his team. Based in London area.
- **Fran Ward** -- Senior Program Manager FLL at IET (FranWard@theiet.org). Advised holding back due to major program changes.
- **Kim Wierman** -- FIRST Inspires (kwierman@firstinspires.org). Received the proposal via email and LinkedIn.
- **Dr. Thameema Ashraf** -- Cc'd on original proposal (dr.ashraf81@gmail.com)

## Status

- **Motion:** win-new / outbound (partnership)
- **Stage:** On hold (advised by IET)
- **Outcome:** Paused pending FLL program restructuring

## Proposal Summary

On 2026-03-29, [Mohamed Anis](../people/mohamed-anis.md) sent a formal Expression of Interest to relaunch FLL in India, citing:

- **3 years of FLL experience** in the UK ecosystem (regionals, nationals, international pathway)
- **Established infrastructure in India** ready to be activated
- **IET model replication** -- leveraging the IET's footprint in India as a strategic backbone, mirroring the UK model
- **India's massive youth demographic** and the gap left when COVID shut down prior FLL operations

The proposal was sent to FIRST's global partnerships team, with a separate forward to Tom Hall at LEGO (who was recently at an AI summit in India) and to Kim Wierman at FIRST Inspires.

## Response

**Tom Hall (2026-03-30):** Forwarded to the responsible team. Noted colleagues on Easter break; asked for patience.

**Fran Ward (2026-03-31):** Advised holding back. The upcoming season (Sep-Mar) will be the final season in the current FLL format. Major changes are coming and nobody knows what lies beyond. Offered to help "further down the line when we know what is happening."
