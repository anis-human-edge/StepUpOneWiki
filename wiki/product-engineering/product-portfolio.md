# Product Portfolio

Complete map of StepUp.One's products and services organized by audience segment. All engines run on the same two-layer methodology: Foundation (strategy, targeting, profile, messaging, AI twin, team) + Daily Execution (connect, engage, pitch, nurture).

**Core thesis:** Human + AI = Outcomes. AI runs research, personalisation, signals, and patterns. Humans run judgment, nuance, and relationships.

## Three Engine Types (Shared Across Products)

Most engines offer three velocity options:

| Engine Type | Volume | Best For |
|-------------|--------|----------|
| **Relationship Led** | 200/month | Long-term trust building, 6-12 month horizon |
| **Intent Led** | 100/month | Targeting based on past behavior and niche alignment |
| **Signal Led** | 100/month | Real-time signal monitoring, engage within 24 hours |

## The Flywheel

The product portfolio forms a closed loop:

1. **[Reskilling Engine](../entities/systems/reskilling-engine.md)** trains underserved populations in AI tools
2. **[Operator Certification](../entities/systems/operator-certification.md)** validates their readiness
3. **[Talent Marketplace](../entities/systems/talent-marketplace.md)** matches them with client engines
4. **Client engines** (Fundraising, GTM, Talent, CXO, etc.) deliver outcomes using these operators
5. Revenue from clients funds more reskilling
