## How this wiki works

This wiki is maintained by an LLM following the [Karpathy wiki pattern](https://gist.github.com/karpathy/442a6bf555914893e9891c11519de94f). Raw sources (meeting transcripts, emails, docs) are farmed automatically from Gmail and Google Drive, then ingested into structured, interlinked wiki pages.

The wiki gets richer with every source added. Cross-references are maintained automatically. Contradictions are flagged. The knowledge compounds.
