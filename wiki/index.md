# StepUp.One Wiki

Company knowledge base for StepUp.One — reskilling and employing the underserved and overlooked worldwide.

## Sections

- **[Training & Reskilling](training-reskilling/)** — The #1 priority. AI curriculum, teaching methodology, and resources for the underserved and overlooked to train themselves.
- **[Clients & Partnerships](clients-partnerships/)** — Win new, serve current, grow existing, win back. Inbound and outbound strategies.
- **[Product & Engineering](product-engineering/)** — Building the StepUp.One platform and tools.
- **[Operations](operations/)** — How we run the company day to day.
- **[Strategy & Vision](strategy-vision/)** — Where we're going and why.
- **[Entities](entities/)** — People, clients, partners, and systems.

## How this wiki works

This wiki is maintained by an LLM following the [Karpathy wiki pattern](https://gist.github.com/karpathy/442a6bf555914893e9891c11519de94f). Raw sources (meeting transcripts, emails, docs) are farmed automatically from Gmail and Google Drive, then ingested into structured, interlinked wiki pages.

The wiki gets richer with every source added. Cross-references are maintained automatically. Contradictions are flagged. The knowledge compounds.
