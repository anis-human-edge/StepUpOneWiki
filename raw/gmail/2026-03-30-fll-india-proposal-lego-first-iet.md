Dear FIRST Global Partnerships Team,

I hope this email finds you well.

I am writing to you on behalf of a dedicated team of FIRST LEGO League (FLL) participants, mentors, and advocates currently based in the UK. Over the past three years, we have had the privilege of deeply engaging with the FLL ecosystem here. We have experienced firsthand the phenomenal job the Institution of Engineering and Technology (IET) does in running the regionals, nationals, and managing the pathway to the international championships.

The impact of this program on students is profound, and it is this exact impact that brings us to our proposal: We want to help relaunch and scale the FIRST LEGO League in India.

We understand that FLL had previously established a presence in India, but unfortunately, the momentum was interrupted, and operations largely closed during the COVID-19 pandemic. Given India's massive youth demographic and rapid technological growth, we believe the FLL program has unprecedented potential there today.

Our team is uniquely positioned to help facilitate this relaunch for the following reasons:

Extensive FLL Experience: Having operated within the highly successful UK framework for nearly three years, we intimately understand the lifecycle of the FLL season, from team registration and regional events to the national and international stages.

Established Infrastructure in India: We currently possess significant on-the-ground infrastructure and networks in India that are ready to be activated.

The IET Model: Because we have seen the success of the IET's infrastructure in the UK, we would love to explore leveraging the IET's existing footprint in India as a strategic backbone for this relaunch, mirroring the highly successful UK model.

We are fully committed to FIRST's core values of Gracious Professionalism and Coopertition. Our primary goal is to bring the joy of robotics, innovation, and teamwork to millions of underserved and eager students across the Indian subcontinent.

We would welcome the opportunity to schedule an introductory call with your International Program Delivery Partner (PDP) development team. We would love to discuss the formal requirements for launching a national FLL program, present our capabilities in more detail, and explore how we can collaboratively bring FIRST back to India in a major way.

Thank you for your time, your dedication to global STEM education, and for creating a program that has inspired us so much. I look forward to hearing from you.

Warm regards,
Anis
