### Summary

Global Innovation Valley discussed pivoting to Artificial Intelligence and aligning with Stepup One for global market success.  
  
**Strategic Shift to AI**  
Global Innovation Valley decided to prioritize Artificial Intelligence as the primary tool for content creation to better serve global clients. This pivot replaces focus on individual software mastery.  
  
**Outcome Based Revenue Model**  
Participants must shift from selling technical skills to delivering high value business outcomes. Success requires professional persistence and proving results to secure global client contracts.  
  
**Integrating Stepup One Operations**  
The organization will adopt Stepup One systems and certifications to gain institutional credibility. This partnership helps scholars learn by managing real client work under professional standards.
