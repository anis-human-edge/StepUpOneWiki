# StepUp.One Daily Learning – 2026/05/01 08:04 BST – Notes by Gemini

Team discussions focused on effective communication, AI-driven commenting strategy, and optimizing engagement for fundraising leads.

**Communication and AI Tool:** Participants emphasized that productive work requires active communication. The team reviewed a new AI commenting tool that automates targeted responses to viral content.

**Amplification Strategy Results:** A demonstration showed that commenting within 2 hours of a post's release generates massive impressions. This strategy leverages AI to provide relevant pitches and drive traffic.

**Tracking and Inbound Strategy:** The team decided to implement universal tracking modules to identify traffic sources. They now prioritize following top fundraising influencers to capture high-quality leads.

## Details

- **Importance of Communication and Action:** Mohamed Anis discussed the importance of speaking up in meetings: "even the mother feeds the kid who cries." Clarified that "talk, do, think" in that order is the framework. People need to talk in meetings, execute work, and think about why things aren't working and improve.

- **AI Breakthrough in Automated Commenting:** Mohamed Anis asked the team if they understood the WhatsApp message about a major breakthrough in commenting. Mahmud Bilow confirmed understanding: the tool generates comments based on viral content, providing two types of comments (with link/amplification, and engagement-only).

- **Understanding the Commenting Tool's Functionality:** When commenting on a viral post using a link, an approximate 1% click-through rate is expected. The AI now contains all 22 landing pages and automatically selects the best, most appropriate link for the content being commented on, which drives viewers to relevant pages.

- **Impact of AI-Powered Amplification Commenting:** AHMED FARHAN highlighted that the AI's ability to understand a trending post and select the appropriate link for a comment can cause an "explosion" of inbound sales. Farhaan Abdi hassan summarized: the system is designed for amplification commenting, where the AI reads the core idea of a trending post, provides an appropriate comment, and leads the person to a relevant landing page.

- **Demonstration of Amplification Strategy Results:** Mohamed Anis demonstrated by showing a comment made on a trending post about security that had been around 15 hours. Their pitch, created two hours after the post's release, related the security topic to fundraising and included a link to their fundraising engine. This comment garnered 45,000 impressions in 14 hours — the highest amount of views Mohamed Anis had ever achieved.

- **Tracking and Optimizing Comment Performance:** Out of 45,000 impressions, 29 people clicked the link in 13 hours. Using a UTM tracker, 11 people came through LinkedIn. UTM tracking per person, per campaign, per channel is possible with the system.

- **Importance of Timely Engagement and Funnel Building:** High impressions were achieved because they immediately responded to comments on the trending post. A sales funnel needs to be built to capture the names of the people who click the link — currently the link sends them directly to the website without capturing names.

- **Timing as a Critical Factor for Impressions:** Commenting on a post that has been around for 17 hours resulted in only 14 impressions, vs. 45,000 impressions achieved by commenting within two hours. The effort required to get 7 impressions is the same as the effort required to get 45,000 impressions. Catching a trending post within the first hour ensures high visibility.

- **AI-Powered Amplification and Engagement Commenting:** The AI reads the post, determines what to amplify, and builds a targeted comment. Two types of outputs:
  1. Amplification comments (include the pitch/link)
  2. Engagement comments (congratulatory, do not sell)
  The AI automatically detects which context is needed.

- **Future Implementation for Tracking User Performance:** Mohamed Anis proposed adding individual team member names to the link's UTM source, such as `/UTM medium equal to Ahmed`. This will allow tracking who is generating the highest amount of traffic.

- **Identifying Trending Posts:** Mohamed Anis proposed identifying the top 100 influencers in startup fundraising (e.g., Y Combinator, TechCrunch), following them, and using notifications to immediately attack their posts upon release. Cautioned against following general influencers even if their posts trend, because their followers may not be the targeted founders needed for high-quality leads — quality over quantity.

- **Inbound Strategy Summary:** The five key elements of the inbound strategy:
  1. Attract (post, comment, blog)
  2. Qualify (questions)
  3. Educate (personal report)
  4. Convince (landing page)
  5. Win the client (booking)
