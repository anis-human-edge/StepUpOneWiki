The company is self-funded with a codebase of close to 590,000 lines of code, designed for scale and to meet future compliance and regulatory issues, particularly in light of European overregulation. The system provides a unique advantage to business operators across fintech, healthcare, and defense.

- **The Dynamic Desk and Execution Control Infrastructure**: The system includes a "dynamic desk" — a near-language interface where users converse with the Presidium instead of using multiple tabs, generating real-time dashboards and information signals. The company has created a new category called "execution control infrastructure." The entire process is auditable, allowing all decisions to be rolled back and documented.

- **AI Agnostic Infrastructure and Product Maturity**: The Presidium is AI agnostic infrastructure — users can plug in any current AI or multiple AIs, supporting up to 400 or 500 sub-AI agents. All six product services are approximately 92-95% built, with the MVP being the execution control infrastructure layer.

- **Funding as a Current Barrier**: Funding is the biggest current barrier, needed for infrastructure, equipment, and marketing cash. The initial demo for investors will focus on communications to show how the system governs and complies through the execution gate, allowing rollback. Everything expected to be demoable within 2-3 weeks.

- **Competitive Advantage and Data Sovereignty**: The company has a 20-page pitch deck prepared. The biggest competitive advantage is sovereignty — the system can run locally (e.g., on a Mac Mini), ensuring data stays controlled and preventing IP siphoning by larger models. Avoids vendor lock-in. Particularly beneficial for European GDPR compliance.
