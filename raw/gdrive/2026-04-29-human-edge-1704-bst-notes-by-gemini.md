- **Introduction of Critical Thinking Initiative**: Mohamed Anis introduced a new initiative focused on teaching critical thinking skills, which they believe is crucial because the world has "lost its thing about critical thinking." The core objective is to train the mind to think critically, which is considered the only relevant skill for children and adults in the age of AI.

- **Curriculum and Critical Thinking Lessons**: The curriculum consists of 20 lessons designed to promote critical thinking, such as asking "who benefits" before believing information, as illustrated by examples like the Roblox pet sale or the energy drink that makes gamers faster. Level one is intended to be offered completely free of charge.

- **Three-Tiered Business Model for Critical Thinking Training**:
  - Level 1: Entirely free. 20 simple lessons that even adults can use to become better critical thinkers.
  - Level 2: $20/month. Higher-level content plus a "hardcore AI" toolkit that allows users to interrogate the AI to bolster their understanding and skills.
  - Level 3: $20/class. Interacting with a human teacher or facilitator to sharpen skills.
