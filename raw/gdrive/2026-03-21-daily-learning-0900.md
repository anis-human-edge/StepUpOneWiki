### Summary

Data presentation review exposed manual data entry and triggered an extended discussion about sustainable CRM dashboard creation and strategy with new AI twin system.  
  
**CRM data input mandate**  
The review of 100 opportunities data presented in an Excel sheet led to a mandate that all data must be managed and viewed within the CRM for consistency. Manual data entry into external dashboards was deemed unsustainable and time-consuming for long-term maintenance.

**New AI twin system introduced**
